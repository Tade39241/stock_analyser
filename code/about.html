About Company Sentiment Analyser


Company Sentiment Analyser is a project designed to track publicly traded companies and analyse their public sentiment.

Our goal is to provide users with valuable insights into company performance and market trends, empowering informed decision-making.

What is the sentiment score?

Every news article has a sentiment score ranging from 0 to 100. This score describes how positive or negative the article is. An article would be described as negative if it has a score much less than 50, positive if it has a score much more than 50, and neutral if it has a score around 50.

How are metrics calculated?

Each companies public perception rating is calculated by taking the average sentiment score of all its associated news articles.
